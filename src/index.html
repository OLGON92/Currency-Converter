Hello, welcome to the epic Currency Converter!

Thank you for joining me! I am a currency converter known as Convertotron. My mission in life is to help humans understand the conversion rate between various earth nations.

I am very easy to use:

  1. Enter the number you would like to convert.
  2. Then choose an ISO 4217 code that represents the initial country you would like to convert from.
  3. Then pick a second ISO 4217 code that represents the country you would like to exchange your currency to.
  4. Example: 125 USD to EUR
